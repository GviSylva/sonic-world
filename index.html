Sonic World

Sonic the Hedgehog is a beloved video game character and franchise created by Yuji Naka, Naoto Ohshima, and Hirokazu Yasuhara for Sega. The franchise follows Sonic, an anthropomorphic blue hedgehog who battles the evil Doctor Eggman (also known as Dr. Robotnik), a mad scientist.
Characters
An antihero, Shadow has good intentions but will do whatever it takes to accomplish his goals, putting him at odds with Sonic the Hedgehog.
Knuckles is determined, serious, and sometimes gullible. He is fiercely loyal to his friends and will go to great lengths to protect them
Tails is loyal, kind and curious. In his early years, he was frequently bullied for his twin tails. His life took a positive turn when he met Sonic, forming an unbreakable bond with him
Eggman is a mad scientist who seeks to steal the mystical Chaos Emeralds, defeat Sonic the Hedgehog, and conquer the world
